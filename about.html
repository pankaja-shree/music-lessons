This website has been designed to initially provide lessons for learning Varanams in Indian Classical Music.
Varanam is a fundamental part of Carnatic Music. Learning Varanam will provide a basic foundation for learners, familiarizing them with the scale of a Raga and all possible combinations of notes in a particular Raga.
For more information on Varanam, visit the wikipedia page.
The notations for Varanams has been taken from the website: www.shivkumar.org
Later more lessons on various other forms of Carnatic Music such as Keerthanas, Devaranamas, etc will be added.

About the Author:

I am a student of carnatic music. The audio clips have been recorded during my music classes when I learnt from my Guru. They have been recorded in such a way that each stanza of a song has been repeated twice or thrice which provides good practise for amateur learners.
